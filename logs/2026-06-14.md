## Canonical additions

### 1. GEO-Bench: Benchmarking Ranking Manipulation in Generative Engine Optimization

- URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29107
- Date: 2026-05-27
- Type: paper + benchmark
- Added to:
  - `data/papers.csv` as `P025`
  - `data/benchmarks.csv` as `B010`
- Why include: This is the strongest new candidate found in this run. It standardizes direct comparison of GEO ranking-manipulation methods under one protocol and evaluates both effectiveness and stealth.
- Key content: Covers black-box prompt attacks including TAP and Zero-Shot, white-box gradient-based attacks including STS, RAF and StealthRank, plus ten white-hat C-SEO strategies. Uses five datasets and a fixed Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct ranker.
- Metrics: NRG, Success@alpha, Promote@alpha, keyword violation rate, perplexity ratio.
- Safety note: This is dual-use. Keep it under `core-safety` and use for defensive benchmarking, detectability analysis and governance, not operational attack guidance.

### Structural Feature Engineering for Generative Engine Optimization

- URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29979
- Date: 2026-03-31
- Decision: watch
- Reason: Relevant because it argues for macro/meso/micro content-structure optimization and reports citation-rate gains. However, it partially overlaps with FeatGEO and GEO Citation Lab style feature analysis, so it should not be promoted before verifying novelty and assets.

### AI Answer Engine Citation Behavior: An Empirical Analysis of the GEO16 Framework

- URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.10762
- Date: 2025-09-13
- Decision: watch
- Reason: Useful observational B2B SaaS citation audit with 70 product-intent prompts, 1,702 citations, 1,100 unique URLs, and a 16-pillar page-quality framework. It is practical but narrower and older than GEO Citation Lab, so keep as measurement-side watch item.

### Caption Injection for Optimization in Generative Search Engine

- URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.04080
- Date: 2025-11-06
- Decision: watch
- Reason: First-pass multimodal G-SEO candidate using image captions injected into textual content for MRAG settings. It is relevant to multimodal GEO but should be checked against newer Pinterest VLM/GEO and multimodal ranker work before canonical promotion.
